About

A quick note from the developer.

Why I made Sieve

Hey — thanks for installing Sieve.

Honestly, I made this because I got tired. The internet I grew up on felt fun and open. Somewhere over the years it turned into scams, nasty comments, endless feeds, and sites that trick you at every click. It stopped feeling safe or clean — for kids, for grown-ups, for pretty much anyone.

So I built the tool I wished existed: something that quietly cleans all that up and gives you back a bit of control. It all runs on your own device — nothing tracked, no account, no catch. That's it.

I just want the internet to feel a little more human again. Hope Sieve helps.

— monolab
